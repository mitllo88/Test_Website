Inspirations

1.1.1.1

1.1.1.1's design is very nice looking. It has a lot of whitespace that is broken up by (and subsequently draws your eye to) the main parts of the site: the logo, the download links and the company link. The logo also moves, drawing your attention to it, you are then also driven down to the arrow, which non-verbally instructs you to scroll down.


Github

Github's design is also nice. The white text on a dark background emphasise the text along with a contrasting login box. The white bottom and partially obscured boxes draw your eyes down to more parts of the site.


AMD

This site is nice because instead of large amounts of whitespace, there is alot of blackspace that is broken up by white text and a white logo as well as a large video that draws your attention to the latest product that they are trying to sell.
